## One command. Zero distractions.

FocusKit is a set of three lightweight terminal scripts that help you focus, stay on track, and keep your machine clean — without installing any apps.

**▸ `startfocus`** — Blocks distracting sites (YouTube, Reddit, X, and more) and starts a focus timer. Your browser literally can't reach them until you're done.

**▸ `endfocus`** — Restores everything when you're ready. Sites come back, timer stops. Clean exit, every time.

**▸ `cleanup`** — Clears out temp files and old downloads clogging up your machine. Shows you exactly what it's removing before it deletes anything.

---

### Why FocusKit?

🚫 **No app to install.** It's just scripts. Copy them, run them, done.

⚡ **One command.** Type `sudo startfocus` and you're in focus mode. That's it.

🔒 **Actually blocks sites.** Not a browser extension that can be disabled in two clicks. FocusKit modifies your system hosts file — there's no workaround short of ending the session.

🧹 **Cleans up real junk.** Temp files, stale downloads, browser caches. See exactly what's being removed, with a confirmation step.

🪶 **Lightweight.** No background processes, no daemons, no electron apps eating your RAM. Just scripts that run when you call them.

🔓 **Fully transparent.** Every script is readable. No obfuscation, no telemetry, no tracking. You can open the files and see exactly what they do.
